Gianluigi Buffon Childhood: Gianluigi Buffon was born on January 28, 1978, in Carrara, Italy, into a family of national-level athletes. His father, Adriano, was a weightlifter, while his mother, Maria, was a discus thrower. Buffon grew up among two sisters, who were also inclined toward sports from a young age. His parents served as inspirations throughout his childhood, with Buffon hitting the field at the age of 5 due to his athletic genes.
